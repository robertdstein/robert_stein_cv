& {\parbox{108mm}{\rule{0pt}{2pt}\small{I am involved in the search of the sources of high-energy neutrinos. This includes on one side the development of  a new muon track reconstruction for high energy neutrino events, that improves the IceCube point spread function by $\sim 30$~\%. On the other side, I performed an analysis to look for  a correlation between high-energy neutrinos and the cores of Active Galactic Nuclei (AGN).
